About Cocoland Homes & Country Farm

Sustainable housing for our
FUTURE GENERATION

Cocoland Homes and Country Farms is more than just a real estate project. Its vision embodies the essence of modern urban living sets against the backdrop of pristine mountains and the tranquil sea located in the heart of the progressive first class Municipality of Sariaya, Quezon. We are a pioneering community dedicated to redefining the concept of sustainable living. Our mission is to blend affordability and luxury into a unique concept we call "Kabahayan at Kabuhayan." In collaboration with the Department of Human Settlements and Urban Development, the LGU of Sariaya Quezon, and the Filipino Land Development Corporation Consortium are committed to creating a place where residents can enjoy the conveniences of modern life while living in harmony with nature. At Cocoland Homes and Country Farms, we pride ourselves on preserving the cultural heritage of the region, especially its ancestral houses which make this locale a remarkable tourism haven with a nod to the Philippines' colonial history. We prioritize accessibility, environmental responsibility, and education offering residents an opportunity to embrace the future of sustainable living. We are driven by innovation, incorporating smart technology, green living practices, and a commitment to self-sufficiency. As a forward-thinking real estate development, our goal is to lead the way of sustainable living in the Philippines. Join us on this remarkable journey to create a better, more sustainable future by investing in Cocoland Homes and Country Farms.


Mission

Our mission at Cocoland Homes and Country Farms is to create a thriving and harmonious community that seamlessly blends modern urban living with the serene beauty of the mountains and sea. We are committed to promoting sustainable living by offering a unique, affordable, and luxurious concept of "Kabahayan at Kabuhayan," which integrates smart technology, green living, and self-sustainability.

Moreover, we are dedicated to addressing the challenges of climate change through responsible land development. This commitment extends to the construction of internet-driven greenhouses and an efficient waste management system, minimizing our ecological footprint. We aim to empower our residents through education by offering a technical school that teaches integrated modern farm living, complemented by a bagsakan center providing access to fresh, locally-sourced food.

Vision

To create a harmonious community at Cocoland Homes and Country Farms where modern urban living seamlessly blends with the serenity of mountain and sea, setting a new standard for sustainable living. Our vision is to offer residents an affordable but luxurious concept of "Kabahayan at Kabuhayan," integrating smart technology, green living, and self-sustainability.

Our vision of a better future also includes innovation, from semi-smart homes to mobile-controlled greenhouses. We aspire to set the benchmark for sustainable living in the Philippines, preserving our environment through a water reservoir, water treatment facility, and a waste management system. We invite you to invest in Cocoland Homes and Country Farms and become a vital part of this journey towards a brighter and more sustainable future.

Cocoland Homes?

Introducing the modern way of urban living within an environment of serene surrounding of mountain and sea. Cocoland Homes and Country Farms is a master plan of sustainable living in a majestic view of Mt. Banahaw, a tourism haven home to the remaining ancestral houses in the Philippines.

Fist Class Municipality

The Cocoland Homes and Country Farms is located in the heart of the progressive first class Municipality of Sariaya, Quezon. It is a 3 hours and 12 minutes drive through the 131 kilometers from Metro Manila via South Luzon Expressway. The municipality continues to maintain its charm, conveying the old Philippines that was shaped by its colonial history. The primary industries of Sariaya are coconut farming and growing vegetables.

Cocoland Home and Country Farms represents internet driven surrounding with its modern greenhouses that houses the entire development, a well-planned sustainable condo unit that would make you proud with its luxurious design and concept, semi smart functionality of a modern home and greenhouse that would bring you an alternative way to earn or plant your own food thru your mobile phone.
